Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How fresh are your flowers?

All our flowers are hand-picked daily from local growers. We guarantee freshness for 7 days or your money back.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! Orders placed before 2 PM can be delivered the same day in select metropolitan areas.

Can I customize my bouquet?

Absolutely! Contact us with your requirements, and our expert florists will create a custom arrangement just for you.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your order, contact us within 48 hours for a full refund or replacement.
