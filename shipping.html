üì¶ Shipping & Delivery

Delivery Areas

We proudly deliver fresh flowers nationwide across the United States. Most metropolitan areas receive expedited service options.

Delivery Times

  • Same-Day Delivery: Available for orders placed before 2:00 PM local time in select metropolitan areas
  • Next-Day Delivery: Orders placed before 8:00 PM are eligible for next-day delivery
  • Standard Delivery: 2-3 business days
  • Scheduled Delivery: Choose a specific delivery date at checkout

Shipping Costs

  • FREE Shipping: On all orders over $50
  • Standard Shipping: $8.00
  • Express Shipping (1-2 days): $15.00
  • Same-Day Delivery: $25.00 (select cities only)

Delivery Process

Our delivery partners will attempt delivery once. If the recipient is unavailable:

  • For residential addresses: Package will be left in a safe location (doorstep, porch) or with a neighbor
  • For business addresses: Package will be left with reception or front desk
  • If delivery is unsuccessful, we will contact you to arrange redelivery

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your delivery in real-time through the provided link.

üåπ Freshness Guarantee

We guarantee that all flowers will arrive fresh and in excellent condition. Our flowers are guaranteed to stay fresh for at least 7 days when properly cared for.

Care Instructions

  • Upon arrival, trim stems at a 45-degree angle
  • Place in clean water with provided flower food
  • Keep away from direct sunlight and heat sources
  • Change water every 2-3 days
  • Remove any wilted flowers to extend the life of the arrangement

Our Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our flowers with a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, we will make it right.

Refund Eligibility

You may request a refund or replacement if:

  • Flowers arrive damaged or wilted
  • You receive the wrong product
  • The arrangement does not match the description
  • Delivery was significantly delayed without notification

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or replacement:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and photos of the product
  3. Describe the issue
  4. We will review and respond within 24 hours

Refund Options

Depending on the situation, we offer:

  • Full Refund: Returned to your original payment method within 5-7 business days
  • Replacement: We'll send a new arrangement at no additional cost
  • Store Credit: Applied to your account for future purchases

Non-Refundable Items

  • Orders refused by the recipient
  • Incorrect delivery address provided by customer
  • Flowers not cared for according to provided instructions
  • Requests made more than 48 hours after delivery

Before Delivery

You may cancel your order for a full refund if:

  • The order has not yet been prepared (typically within 2 hours of placement)
  • For scheduled deliveries, cancellation is made at least 24 hours before the delivery date

After Preparation

Once your order has been prepared and is en route, cancellations are not possible. However, you may refuse delivery for a partial refund (minus shipping costs).
